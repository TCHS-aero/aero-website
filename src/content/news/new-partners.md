We're thrilled to welcome three new partners to TCHS Aero this season: TCSF — TCUSD's generous aid; FrSky and Hitec — industry leaders in RC control systems and servo technology.

Their support will directly fund transmitter systems and servo motors for the upcoming competition season. We're incredibly grateful for their investment in student aerospace education.

If your organization is interested in partnering with TCHS Aero, please visit our [Sponsor page](/sponsor) or reach out directly at tchsaero@tcusd.net.
